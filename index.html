💡 How to view your hologram

1

Save the file

Tap "Save Hologram File" — it saves to your device and stays in My Holograms above for re-download any time.

2

Build the pyramid

Cut 4 trapezoid panels from clear plastic (a CD case works great). Tape them into a 4-sided pyramid — no top or bottom.

3

Place on your screen

Open the file full-screen on your phone. Set the pyramid's small opening face-down in the center of the screen.

4

Watch the hologram

Dim the lights. Look through each face of the pyramid — the floating 3D image will appear.
