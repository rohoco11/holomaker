💡 How to view your hologram

1

Download the file

Tap "Save to Photos" to download your hologram image or video.

2

Build a pyramid

Cut 4 identical trapezoid panels from a clear plastic sheet or CD case. Tape them into a 4-sided pyramid shape (no top or bottom).

3

Place on your screen

Open the downloaded file in full-screen on your phone. Set the pyramid's small opening face-down in the center of the screen.

4

Watch the hologram

Dim the lights and look through each side of the pyramid — the floating 3D effect will appear!
